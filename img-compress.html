Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to the number of images I can compress?

No, you can compress as many images as you need. However, very large batches may take longer to process.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. We don't upload or store your images.

What's the maximum image size I can compress?

There's no strict limit, but very large images (over 20MB) may take longer to process.

Which format should I choose for the best compression?

WebP generally offers the best compression with good quality. JPG is best for photos, while PNG is ideal for graphics with transparency.
